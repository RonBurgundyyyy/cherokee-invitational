Tournament Rules

Buy-in, bonus jackpots, and scorecard for Cherokee Invitational '25.

Main Pot

$50 / Person Buy-In

$600 pot. Team with the lowest score splits the pot.

Bonus Jackpots
-5
Lowest Carded Score

Applied before bonuses.

-3
Hole 9 - Closest to Pin

Must settle on the green to count.

-3
Hole 14 - Longest Drive

Must be in the fairway to count.

-3
Hole 18 - "Happy" Hole

Longest drive, in the fairway, while wearing the Gilmore jersey.

-1
Fireball Holes

Each qualifying Fireball hole earns one stroke.

Fine Print
  • Fireball holes require a birdie. The player who sinks the birdie putt must take a shot of Fireball to claim the bonus.
  • Each team must use two different players' shots at least once per hole.
